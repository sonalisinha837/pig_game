How to play?

  1. Roll the dice and accumulate as many points as you can.
  2. If you roll a 1, then you'll lose all the points that you currently accumulated. Also, next player will get the turn.
  3. Hold the points whenever you like. But after that, next player will get the turn.
  4. The first player to accumulate 100 or more points wins the game.
